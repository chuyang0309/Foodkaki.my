# Foodkaki.my
Website URL: https://talucas1220.github.io/Food-website/

A modern, responsive web application designed to help users explore, understand, and celebrate the rich tapestry of Malaysian food culture. From iconic street foods to traditional heritage cuisines, Foodkaki.my serves as a digital culinary guide for food enthusiasts.

* **Culture & Cuisine Exploration:** Discover the history, ingredients, and unique background of diverse Malaysian dishes and street foods.
* **Dynamic Search:** Quickly search through curated food lists to find specific delicacies or browse by categories.
* **Personalized Favorites:** A built-in bookmarking system that allows users to "favorite" their top dishes and save them for quick reference.
* **Responsive Design:** A fully optimized, mobile-friendly interface built for seamless browsing across desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
